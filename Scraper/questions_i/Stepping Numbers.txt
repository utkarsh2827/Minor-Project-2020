
Problem DescriptionGiven A and B you have to find all stepping numbers in range A to B.
The stepping number:
A number is called as a stepping number if the adjacent digits have a difference of 1.
e.g. 123 is stepping number, but 358 is not a stepping numberInput FormatFirst argument is an integer A.
Second argument is an integer B.Output FormatReturn a integer array sorted in ascending order denoting the stepping numbers between A and B.Example InputInput 1:

 A = 10
 B = 20
Example OutputOutput 1:

 [10, 12]
Example ExplanationExplanation 1:

 All stepping numbers are 10 , 12
